Inflation


Economic growth shows an increase in the production of goods and services in an economy. One way to see the level of the economy is through inflation. Bank Indonesia defines inflation as a general and continuous increase in nutrient prices. An increase in the price of goods and services can be called inflation if the increase extends or affects the price increase of other goods. With this, inflation can reduce the value of money against the value of goods and services in general. It is important to maintain stable inflation because the inflation rate affects economic growth which also has an impact on improving the welfare of the community.

This study uses an Artificial Neural Network to predict the inflation rate of the following month. The data used for this research is inflation data in Indonesia from December 2011 to December 2019 issued by the Central Statistics Agency (BPS).
